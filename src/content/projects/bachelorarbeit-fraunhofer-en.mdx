## What it's about

I wrote my bachelor's thesis at the [Fraunhofer Institute for Solar Energy Systems](https://www.ise.fraunhofer.de/en.html) in Freiburg, Germany. The ISE is one of the world's leading institutes in solar research and integrated energy systems — the combination of real research environment and industry application shaped how I think about work.

## Topic

Data-driven methods for analysis and forecasting in the context of energy systems. The source files (LaTeX) are [on GitHub](https://github.com/Spla4sH/Bachelorarbeit-Fraunhofer) — happy to discuss in more depth.

## What I took away

- Scientific writing — precise, properly referenced, reproducible
- Working with real, messy energy measurement data
- The value of strong baselines before reaching for complex models
- The bridge between research and industrially applicable solutions
